1. Business Model & Moat

8.0 / 10
Measures the company's core operations, monetization streams, and structural competitive barriers.
Key Focus: Optical Datacom Modules (800G, 1.6T transceivers) | Engineered Materials | High-Precision Lasers
Coherent designs and manufactures high-performance optical components, subsystems, and engineered materials vital to global internet data transmission networks. Following its transformational merger with II-VI, the enterprise commands market-leading scale in high-speed optical transceivers required to alleviate data transmission latency inside multi-cluster AI datacenters. Defensive barriers are supported by proprietary crystalline material formulations and extensive fabrication expertise, making the firm an integrated component partner to top-tier semiconductor infrastructure suppliers and cloud hyperscalers.
